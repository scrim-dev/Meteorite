What it offers

Simple clip downloader, no bloat.

Paste a Medal link, get the video. That's basically it. But there's a few nice things on top.

Quick Downloads

Paste a Medal.tv link and the clip downloads straight to your PC. No accounts, no signups.

Watermark Free

You get the raw video, not the one with the Medal branding stamped on it.

Download History

Keeps track of what you've grabbed so you can find, rewatch, or open clips later.

Auto Download

Turn it on and it watches your clipboard. Copy a Medal link and it starts downloading on its own.
